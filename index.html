Village Rules

Covid-19 Measures

The Department of Human Settlements and Urban Development in a memorandum dated 01 May 2020 empowered homeowners’ associations to closely regulate the ingress to and egress from their villages to mitigate the spread of the COVID19 virus. This, together with IATF and local government regulations, guided our work resulting in the release of at least eight (8) circulars on Village Covid-19 Measures within a period of six months in 2020. Our work continues and we kindly ask you to work with us in remaining vigilant. May we kindly remind you that Batangas province remains under GCQ and that our Village Covid-19 measures remain in effect.

Vehicle Sticker Policy

Stickers and all types of passes issued are the property of Terrazas de Punta Fuego Village Homeowners’ Association, Inc (TPFVHAI). Stickers and guest/vehicle passes are extended as privileges. This privilege may be immediately withdrawn due to association members and his/her representative, contractors, and village employees’ failure to comply with village traffic rules/regulations and all other pertinent village policies. All applicants (renewal/new) will be subject to verification. TPFVHAI has the right to refuse the issuance/sale of stickers. Only vehicles with valid TPFVHAI vehicle sticker shall have immediate access inside the village.



About Us

Azure Sands Beach Resort was the result of the partnership forged in September 2001 between Fuego Land Corporation, the joint venture development company of Landco Pacific Corporation and Roxaco Land Corporation, together with the Pedrosa family, to develop their 43-hectare estate originally known as the White Sands Resort in Barangay Natipuan, Nasugbu, Batangas. White Sands has always been known for its pristine waters, the magnificent views of the South China Sea and its forest cover that is home to a variety of Philippine bird species. Terrazas de Punta Fuego continues to live up to its reputation as one of the Philippines’ most exclusive, prime, seaside residential enclaves.

Azure Sands Beach Resort consists of four hundred – sixty one (461) residential lots. Today, there are sixty-two (62) Spanish Mediterranean – inspired homes built. These are further enhanced by Asian-tropical influences. Construction of new homes and the review and approval of designs are on-going. The Village is also home to the Terrazas Beach Club, the Amara en Terrazas Condominium, a number of parks, a forest reserve, and nature trails. The village maintains it own basketball and tennis courts.




The design of its rich natural landscape centered on wellness to provide each family a veritable haven and a well-deserved respite from the usual busy city life.





Azure Sands Beach Resort is currently managed and operated by world-renowned property management firm, Colliers International.
